Cabinet Dismissal — composite mix notes
=========================================
Layers:

  0ms      chair-slide.mp3       loudnorm I=-18 TP=-2 LRA=11
  700ms    door-open-close.mp3   loudnorm I=-18 TP=-2 LRA=11
           (this file already contains a natural open-then-close
            sequence, trimmed from a longer raw recording — see
            audio-source/raw/foley/door-open-close.mp3, atrim 6.9:15.2)

Mixed with ffmpeg amix (duration=longest). Total length: ~9.0s.
Deliberately no sting, no music cue, nothing added after — the
recording just ends where the door-close recording ends. Do not add
anything here; the silence is the point.

Fires only when a Cabinet slot is actively FIRED (view.resigned === false
in GameClient.tsx's handlePickReplacement) — a resignation gets no
special sting.

Rebuild command:
  ffmpeg -i chair-slide.mp3 -i door-open-close.mp3 \
    -filter_complex "[0:a]loudnorm=I=-18:TP=-2:LRA=11[a0];\
                      [1:a]adelay=700|700,loudnorm=I=-18:TP=-2:LRA=11[a1];\
                      [a0][a1]amix=inputs=2:duration=longest:dropout_transition=0[aout]" \
    -map "[aout]" -q:a 3 cabinet-dismissal-master.mp3
